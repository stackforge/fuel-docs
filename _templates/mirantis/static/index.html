Mirantis OpenStack Documentation

With Mirantis OpenStack 7.0, Mirantis responds to the needs of our largest cloud customers running mission-critical workloads with the most stable OpenStack distribution that has been tested extensively, includes features to simplify operations at web-scale and makes it easier to deploy workloads on OpenStack. Based on the Kilo release, Mirantis OpenStack also includes extensive eco-system support including new features for VMWare.
